Be Prepared, Stay Safe

Know your evacuation route, prepare an emergency kit, and stay informed through official channels. During emergencies, follow instructions from barangay officials and CDRRMO personnel.

Emergency Kit

Water, food, flashlight, batteries, first aid kit, whistle, and important documents.

Evacuation Route

Plan at least two routes to your evacuation center. Practice with your family.

Stay Informed

Monitor official announcements via radio, social media, and barangay officials.
