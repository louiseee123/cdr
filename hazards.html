Earthquake Preparedness Tips

What to do before, during, and after an earthquake.

Before an Earthquake

  • Secure heavy furniture and appliances to walls
  • Know the location of your evacuation center
  • Prepare an emergency go-bag with essentials
  • Identify safe spots in each room (under sturdy tables)
  • Keep emergency numbers accessible
  • Participate in community earthquake drills

During an Earthquake

  • DUCK down to the floor
  • COVER your head and neck
  • HOLD ON to your shelter
  • Stay away from windows and glass
  • If outdoors, move to an open area
  • Do not use elevators

After an Earthquake

  • Check yourself and others for injuries
  • Be prepared for aftershocks
  • Check for gas leaks and electrical damage
  • Listen to radio for emergency updates
  • Use stairs when evacuating buildings
  • Keep streets clear for emergency vehicles

Remember: Duck, Cover, and Hold

DUCK

Get down to the ground

COVER

HOLD

Stay in position until shaking stops
